Roles & Permissions
-------------------
Prompted AI with explicit role definitions:
- Creator: create, edit, submit tariffs
- Approver: approve/reject pending tariffs
- Viewer: view approved tariffs only

AI Mistake & Fix
----------------
AI initially restricted Creators to viewing only contracts containing their own tariffs.

Fix:
- Show all contracts to Creators
- Restrict editing/submission to creator-owned tariffs
- Calculate creator-specific tariff counts separately

Where I Verified Manually
-------------------------

Duplicate Validation Decision
-----------------------------
Validation performed during APPROVAL rather than SUBMISSION.

Reason:
- Allows multiple creators to work simultaneously
- Supports concurrent submissions
- Matches assignment requirements

Trade-off:
- More flexibility for creators
- Conflict resolved only when tariff becomes active
